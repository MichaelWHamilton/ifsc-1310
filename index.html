Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progress enhancement is a modern web design methodology. With so many tools available these days that offer customization to web design, this methodolgy is meant to ensure that the content of a web app reaches all users first, regardless of their platform. The way I see it is, this method is meant to make sure that the foundation of a web app comes first, no matter the platform you may be viewing content on. This is important because not everyone has access to resources that allow them to use advanced browser features and augmented content. Once the foundation has been layed for all, then any further enhancement to the web app is gravy. An article I read, describes progressive enhancement as the rule of "separation of presentation and content, separation of content and style, or separation of semantics and presentation. https://blog.logrocket.com/understanding-progressive-enhancement/ . I couldn't say it any better. As long as the core material is there, all the extra stuff is just a bonus.
